Share via Email

Diagrams can easily be emailed to other people through Architexa. Following are the steps to easily email diagrams

  1. Once you have created a diagram click the Architexa Collaboration () icon in the toolbar and select the "Share Diagram" option or go to "File > Share Diagram"
  2. This will pop up a dialog box with tabs "Upload To Server" and "Email As Attachment".
  3. Select the "Email As Attachment" tab
  4. Populate the dialog with the required information about the diagram (Diagram Name, Description and Tags).
  5. Enter the email addresses of the contacts to whom the diagram needs to be sent.
  6. Multiple email addresses can be added (semi-colon separated) when a diagram has to be sent to more than one person.
  7. Email addresses can also be accessed from the address book saved on the server.
  8. Once you have added all the information click 'Share' to send the diagram.
