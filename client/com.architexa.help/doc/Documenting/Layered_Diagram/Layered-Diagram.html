Documenting with Layered Diagrams

When creating documentation in a layered diagram the following tips may be helpful.

  1. It's easier to start from the top down. Start at the project level and expand important packages.
  2. Right click on the project and open in a layered diagram
  3. Expand the diagram one layer at a time
  4. Remove packages that are not pertinent to your code concept
  5. Larger text typically contains more items. Be sure to expand when necessary by double clicking
  6. When the appropriate layers are showing feel free tow drag packages and classes to beautify the diagram and make it easier to understand. (Be careful as this may result in dependent layers being below their dependees)
  7. You may now select the classes and/or packages you would like to show dependency information for. Do this by holding ctrl while clicking on individual items.
