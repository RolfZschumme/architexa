Keep documentation up to date

The following steps will allow you to update and existing diagram with any recent changes.

  1. Log in to codemaps.org and join a group
  2. Or have someone who shared a diagram on our secure server invite you to their group
  3. Retrieve the diagram you want to update.
  4. You will notice that portions of the diagram that are no longer accurate will have a blue triangle icon or have connections with a red box.
  5. Update these portions of the diagrams with any changes that have been made
  6. Add comments to explain any changes that have been made
'''NOTE''' You must have the same codebase opened in your workspace as the one the diagram was created in.
