Retrieve diagrams

Architexa client allows easy access to diagrams which have been shared on the collaboration servers. To open a diagram in your client follow the steps bellow:

  1. Click the Architexa Collaboration () icon in the toolbar and go to the "Shared Diagrams" option.
  2. You will see the list of all the groups you have access to in the submenu.
  3. Go to the parent group and select the diagram you want to view
  4. The diagram will now open in your eclipse window.
'''NOTE:''' If your current workspace does not contain the source code <br/>of the opened diagram, some information may not be available.
