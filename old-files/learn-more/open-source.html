Architexa Open Source Initiative

We are working hard to help developers easily understand and document code. While working with Open Source Software we have realized that documentation is a big issue which further makes understanding of these software harder for other developers.

We are great supporters of Open Source Software and want to contribute to it by giving free licenses to developers working with these projects.

Open Source Community (Ambassadors)

Do you use Open Source projects? Do you want to be an ambassador and help others understand the project? You can use Architexa to contribute to the community and easily provide documentation. We are providing free licenses if you are willing to upload good documentation, add comments and vote on good diagrams every week. Oh yeah, help us by sending your feedback about the architexa suite.

What you need to do

  1. Create an account on Codemaps.
  2. Send us an e-mail to support@architexa.com with (a) your account information (email with which you registered), (b) the Open Source project you are working with, (c) what you plan to use Architexa to help you with and we will enable your license for the client (download here).
  3. Send us feedback at feedback@architexa.com. We want to keep improving the suite to perform even better for you.
