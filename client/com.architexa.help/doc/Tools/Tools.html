Diagramming Tools

Architexa consists of three main diagramming tools. Layered diagrams help you get an overview of large codebases and quickly get a feel for unfamiliar code. Class diagrams assist with understanding and documenting the important relationships between classes. Sequence Diagrams are good for diving into the detailed logic of classes or methods.

  • Layered Diagram
  • Class Diagram
  • Sequence Diagram
