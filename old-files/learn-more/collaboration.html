Easy Collaboration

We want to make it easy for you to share diagrams with your team members. We have built in support for collaboration in Architexa.


Collaborate and Share Using:


Source Repository Save diagrams locally and then upload them to your source repository to provide access for the entire team. They can then open and explore your diagrams in Eclipse. Included
Email Create diagrams and share them with colleagues immediately using our built in email support. We also provide an address book so that you can easily manage your contacts. Included
Shared Collaboration Server Use our built in one-click sharing to add your diagram to our server so that anyone you invite can easily access and comment on it from anywhere in the world. Included
Group Server For extra security contact us and we can set up a local server to run on your intranet. This will provide all the same functionaity of our cloud server while keeping your data local. See the collaboration section of the Pricing Page
